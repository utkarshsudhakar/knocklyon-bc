The new season is here and we're delighted to welcome everyone back to Knocklyon Community Centre for another year of competitive badminton.

## What's new this season

This year we're fielding **five teams** across the Leinster leagues — our strongest representation yet. Training nights remain on **Mondays, Tuesdays, and Thursdays**, with the first official club night taking place this Tuesday.

## Fixtures

The full fixture list for all teams is now available on our Fixtures page. Check it regularly as dates will be confirmed throughout the season.

## New members

If you're Division 7 or above and looking for a competitive club, now is the perfect time to get in touch. Spots are limited so don't leave it too late — reach out via the Contact page and we'll get you in for a session.

Here's to a great season ahead. See you on the court! 🏸
