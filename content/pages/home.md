## Who are we?

Knocklyon Badminton Club is a competitive badminton club based in Knocklyon, South Dublin. We take our badminton seriously — fielding teams across multiple grades in the Leinster Badminton leagues and cups, with players who show up each week ready to push themselves and each other.

We're a tight-knit club with high standards on the court and a great atmosphere off it. If you love the game and want to play it at a decent level, you'll fit right in.

## What we offer

**Leinster League competition** — we compete across Men's, Ladies', and Mixed grades. Matches are played at a competitive level and every player is expected to contribute to their team.

**Club nights** — open to all members, Tuesdays and Thursdays are where the club comes together. High-quality social play, strong rallies, and the kind of games that keep you coming back.

**Team practice** — Mondays are dedicated team sessions for squad players preparing for league fixtures.

**A club that means business** — small enough to know everyone, serious enough to keep standards high.

## Joining the club

We welcome players of **Division 7 and above**. If you're at that level and looking for a competitive club with a strong league programme and a good social side, we'd love to hear from you.

Get in touch and we'll arrange for you to come along to a session.
